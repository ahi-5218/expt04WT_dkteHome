D.K.T.E. Society's Textile & Engineering Institute
An Empowered Autonomous Institute
Affiliated to Shivaji University, Kolhapur.

DKTE’s Textile & Engineering Institute, Ichalkaranji enjoys a unique and prominent place amongst the Engineering colleges that are engaged in education, training, research, and consultancy in various disciplines of engineering. DKTE has been founded in 1982. Institute has been catering to Diploma, Graduate, Post-Graduate, and Doctorate programs in Textile, Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Computer Science & Engineering, Artificial Intelligence and Data Science, Electrical Engineering, Civil Engineering, and Management Studies (MBA). We have around 4000 students and 215 faculty members.


Institute was started in the year 1982 and is the First Institute in the state of Maharashtra started on a no-grant basis. The Institute has achieved remarkable milestones and glorious achievements, a few of them -

1. The institute is running, 3 Diploma, 12 UG, 06 PG, and 04 Ph.D. programs.

2. Received autonomy in 2016 and we received Empowered Autonomy from Shivaji University in 2023.

3. Institute is NAAC accredited with A+ Grade consequently for two cycles.

4. Institute’s almost all programs are NBA accredited. One of the programs has received 6 years of accreditation. In western Maharashtra, ours is the only Institute that got 6 years of accreditation.

5. For the last three years consecutively the Institute has been figured in NIRF innovation Ranking at the National level.

6. The institute has emerged as the Winner of ‘Best Industry-Linked Engineering Institute”- three times (in year 2015,2017,2021) in the nationwide survey conducted jointly by AICTE and CII.

7. The institute has been awarded many prestigious funding projects from Govt. of India, amounting Rs 60 Cr+

8. The institute has signed MoUs with 29 Foreign Universities and 100+ industries, which indicates a very good Industry liaison.

9. Institute has been identified to act as a “Mentor Institution” under AICTE’s Margdarshak Scheme and also as a “Potential Mentor” by UGC under PARAMARSH scheme to promote Quality and Accreditation under UGC.

Vision

To be one of the leading institutes by integrating learning, research, and entrepreneurship into holistic education, fostering innovative solutions for industrial and societal challenges utilizing state-of-the-art technology.

Mission

M1: To enhance academic standards by integrating technological advancements into the curriculum, pedagogy and assessment.

M2: To facilitate research and innovation by providing a holistic and conducive learning environment.

M3: To develop industry ready graduates having concern for society and environment, imbibed with ethical behaviour and equipped with professional skills and entrepreneurship abilities by enhancing collaboration with industries, academic and research institutes.
